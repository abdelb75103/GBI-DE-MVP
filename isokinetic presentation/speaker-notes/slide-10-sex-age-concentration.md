# Slide: Who The Evidence Actually Describes

## Current Sequence Position
Immediately after the extraction framework slide and before the later problem-framing slide.

## Purpose
Show that the evidence base is not evenly distributed across the game. The main point is not simply that men appear more often. The point is that the literature is still concentrated around senior men, with much thinner coverage for senior women and incomplete coverage for youth football.

## Speaking Cues & Reveal Order

* **Headline appears**
  * **Say:** "Once the papers are in extraction, we can ask a more practical question: who does this evidence actually describe?"

* **Top strip appears**
  * **Action:** Click/wait for the men’s and women’s metrics strip.
  * **Say:** "Across the full export, 304 papers include men’s metrics, compared with 110 that include women’s metrics. Papers with both are counted in both groups."

* **Senior men bar appears**
  * **Action:** Click/wait for the first bar.
  * **Say:** "The largest concentration is still senior men."

* **Youth football bar appears**
  * **Action:** Click/wait for the second bar.
  * **Say:** "Youth football is represented, but not at the same depth."

* **Senior women bar appears**
  * **Action:** Click/wait for the third bar.
  * **Say:** "Senior women remain much less represented in the extracted literature."

* **Closing line appears**
  * **Action:** Click/wait for the takeaway band.
  * **Say:** "So the imbalance is not subtle. Senior men account for about 150 papers here, compared with 41 for senior women, roughly a 3.7-fold difference. That is exactly the kind of concentration this living system needs to make visible."

## Key Notes
- Counts are paper-level and inclusive:
  - papers with both men and women are counted in both sex categories
- Current figures used on slide:
  - men’s metrics: 304 papers
  - women’s metrics: 110 papers
  - senior men: 150 papers
  - senior women: 41 papers
  - youth football: 113 papers
- These are preliminary export figures and should be treated as directional until the final cleaned analysis set is locked.
