# Benchmarking Goals

- Need a benchmark that compares single-model writing against a merged dual-model workflow.
- Want the process to be understandable by internal stakeholders, not just ML specialists.
- Need a mix of generic writing tasks and realistic internal prompts.
- Prefer a small benchmark that can be rerun after prompt or workflow changes.
- Quality bar should favor faithfulness and usefulness over flashy prose.
- Human review should focus on high-priority prompts and close calls.
