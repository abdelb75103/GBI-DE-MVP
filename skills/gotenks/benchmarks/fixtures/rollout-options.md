# Rollout Options

Option A: Open beta next week
- Pros: Fast feedback, more usage data, momentum for launch.
- Cons: Support burden rises immediately, onboarding polish is not complete.

Option B: Expand to 20 design partners first
- Pros: Controlled feedback loop, easier to support, better signal on high-value use cases.
- Cons: Slower growth, may feel conservative to leadership.

Option C: Delay for four weeks and launch broadly
- Pros: More polished experience, better docs, fewer obvious paper cuts.
- Cons: Risk of losing current momentum and partner interest.

Current constraints:
- One engineer available for support rotation.
- Docs refresh can be completed in ten days.
- Sales wants at least five customer references before broad launch.
