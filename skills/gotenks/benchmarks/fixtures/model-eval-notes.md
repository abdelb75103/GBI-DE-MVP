# Dual-LLM Pilot Notes

- Tested 27 writing prompts across reports, memos, summaries, and action plans.
- Codex drafts were usually tighter on structure and next steps.
- Claude drafts were usually stronger on tone and narrative flow.
- Best final outputs came from consensus-first synthesis rather than naive concatenation.
- Biggest failure mode: when one draft hallucinated a detail, the merger sometimes repeated it unless the synthesis prompt was strict.
- Team wants a recommendation on whether to ship the merged workflow as the default or keep it as an advanced option.
