# Retrospective Findings

- Team spent too much time reformatting model output by hand.
- Research links were inconsistent and sometimes missing.
- Handoffs between PM and ops were delayed because action items were buried in long summaries.
- The synthesis step produced the best output when both drafts saw the same context packet.
- Biggest request from users: a faster way to compare one-model output against the merged final version.
- Known constraint: no one wants a heavy dashboard for v1.
